Tyler Garrett

Artist and Photographer

In 2017, I was struggling with depression and an early mid-life crisis (although I was only 27). This state forced me to begin a journey of transformation. I wanted to grow into the best version of myself and discover what brings me true “joi”. Thus, the concept of Finding My Joi was birthed.

Finding My Joi started as a podcast where I shared my life experiences in hopes to help others going through the same struggles. Along this journey I discovered my true passion for God, helping others, art, and photography. This website is a collaboration of my creations which promote these passions.

Welcome to Finding My Joi! My ultimate goal is to spread “joi” and to help others find their “joi”.

To learn more about my work history, check out my resume. Thanks for stopping by!
